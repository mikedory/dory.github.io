Hi! I'm Mike. I might have said that already, but...

 

Who are you?

I'm a technologist, writer, and artist. I like to build things that real people can use to explore their relationships and the world around them.

I like to take hikes, shoot pictures, and ride boards. I really like coffee, too.

What do you do?

All sorts of stuff! I co-founded Socialbomb, a social technology agency based in Brooklyn. I've taught at places like NYU ITP and General Assembly, and spoken at places like O'Reilly OSCon and the Mobile Gaming Summit.

Most recently, I co-wrote a book for O'Reilly Media called Introduction to Tornado, which is out now!

Hey, I want to get in touch with you!

That sounds rad! You can:

  • read my witty banter on Twitter
  • check out my timeline on Facebook
  • fork my latest work on Github
  • do... professional stuff... on Linkedin

But for most things, you can also just email me. It would be cool to hear from you!

So, how did you make this thing?

This site was built using Hyde and Markdown, with a few Python tools thrown in for good measure. SCSS and Coffeescript handle styles and scripting. It is served via Ruby/Rack on Heroku, and the source is up on Github.
