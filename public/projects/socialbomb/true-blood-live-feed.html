True Blood Live Feed

With the True Blood Live Feed, you can link your copy of True Blood Season 2 on Blu-ray directly to your Facebook & Twitter accounts. As you watch, your picture will transform. Your updates will change, according to your viewing habits.

The first BD-Live™ app that allows users to share scenes with their Blu-ray remote to their Facebook and Twitter Feed. Customized status updates, unlockable virtual gifts, and profile picture transformations extend the world of the show and fuel the fandom.

True Blood Live Feed was developed for HBO’s television show True Blood, with BBDO, Technicolor and Deluxe.

Check out the app on Facebook, and start feeding!
