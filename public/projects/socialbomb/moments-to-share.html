Moments to Share

It is with equal parts pride and excitement that we at Socialbomb announce here the launch of Fisher-Price Moments to Share, the app we’ve been hard at work on over the past months.

Moments to Share is a Facebook application that lets users record their children’s special moments in a dynamic timeline of digital photos, videos, captions and stories, and then share them with friends and family.

We’re thrilled to see this go out into the wild, and we’re looking forward to seeing to seeing how people use it.

Some folks are already writing about the app: - WSJ covers Fisher-Price Moments to Share on Facebook - eMedia Vitals covers Fisher-Price Moments to Share & discusses the efficacy of Facebook app development

Check out the application on Facebook and start your own timeline!
